# Community Standards

WaveX OS is an open-source project that welcomes contributors of all backgrounds.
We hold ourselves to a professional standard in every interaction — issues, pull
requests, discussions, and any other project space.

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a respectful and
productive experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, background,
or identity. Maintainers and contributors are expected to uphold these standards.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive in technical disagreements.
- Give and receive feedback graciously — critique the idea, not the person.
- Help newcomers find their footing and ask clarifying questions before assuming intent.
- Acknowledge when you are wrong; update your position based on evidence.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behaviour

Conduct that is unwelcome in this project includes — but is not limited to —
personal attacks, derogatory language, deliberate intimidation, and any form of
discrimination. Maintainers will remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments
that violate these standards, and may ban repeat offenders.

## Reporting

If you witness or experience behaviour that violates these standards, email
**dylanriedw10@icloud.com**. Reports are reviewed confidentially. All maintainers
are obligated to respect reporter privacy.

## Enforcement

Maintainers who do not enforce these standards in good faith may face repercussions
as determined by other project leadership.

## Scope

These standards apply in all project spaces (GitHub Issues, PRs, Discussions, and
any official communication channel) and when an individual represents the project
in public.
